Become A Home Hero!

At Home Services isn’t your average home repair company. We don’t hire 9-5 employees to turn a wrench, we contract with skilled business owners, giving them valuable tools and marketing know-how, allowing them to focus on growing their business and creating life-long customers. As an At Home Hero, you’ll work in your own area, have first choice of jobs within set zip codes, and get to control your own schedule. You can take all the time off in the world for that vacation you planned, or take as much work as you can handle. It’s your business and your earning power. We’ll be there to support you.

Hi, my name is Chris Miller.

I’m the owner of At Home Services, and your personal resource in building your business. I’ve been a home repair techinician my whole career, and I know how difficult it can be to balance turning a wrench, scheduling, and marketing all at once.

It seems there have always been two ways to operate a home repair business: stay small and take whatever jobs come your way due to a lack of ressources and marketing know- how, or get hired by a large company that makes you clock in everyday and writes your paycheck

I think there should be a third way, which is why I created At Home Services. Instead of hiring technicians like Whipple or Action Plumbing, I want to give guys just like me a chance to grow their own business, while remaining in control of their schedules and income.

As a franchisee of At Home Services, you’ll have tools at your disposal the average repairman in the KSL Classifieds doesn’t, with a mobile app connected to a database that allows you to invoice, send emails, enter customers, and see an overview of your progress any time you like. Our office, called the Guardianship, is staffed by talented dispatchers and marketers, all working to support you in your professional endeavors. We have myriad strategies and marketing techniques to make your own work benefit you.
